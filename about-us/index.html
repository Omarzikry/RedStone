About Us

What is a RedStone?

A Red stone is not simply the stone of a redelephant. It is the name of the most common knot used for zipper pulls. Why name our company after a knot?

We Are: Detail. Simplicity. Strength.

The Redstone knot is small but powerful. We focus on small details because they are what make powerful products.

The Redstone knot is beautiful in its simplicity, which is our approach to design. We create minimalistic apparel focused on fit, form, and function.

The harder you pull the Redstone knot, the tighter it becomes. We aim to bring people together and strengthen bonds, fighting against the forces that pull us apart.
